What is Texas Hold 'em?

If you are familiar with the game, you can proceed directly to joining section.

Texas Hold 'em is one of the most popular variants of the card game of poker. Two cards, known as hole cards, are dealt face down to each player, and then five community cards are dealt face up in three stages. Each player seeks the best five card poker hand from any combination of the seven cards; the five community cards and their two hole cards. Players can raise the bet or fold (quit the round) every stage (as well as check and call), and the last player that didn't fold wins the pot.
